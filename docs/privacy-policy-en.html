Privacy Policy

Mental Gym  ·  Last updated: March 15, 2026

Summary: Mental Gym does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data is stored exclusively on your device.

1. Who We Are

Mental Gym is a mobile app that supports daily mental training — creativity, critical thinking, and mindfulness. The app is available on Android.

2. What Data We Process

Mental Gym does not require an account or any personal information. The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Notes — text entries you write for exercises
  • Photos — photos attached to notes (taken with camera or selected from gallery)
  • Progress — information about completed exercises and streaks
  • Settings — chosen theme (dark / light) and language (PL / EN)
  • Training plans — custom weekly plans created by the user

All data is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (AsyncStorage) and local file system. None of this data is transmitted to external servers.

3. Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera — to take photos to attach to notes. Photos are saved locally on your device.
  • Gallery / Storage access — to select existing photos from your device. Selected photos are copied locally.
  • Notifications — to send optional morning training reminders. You can disable them in system settings at any time.

We will not use any of these permissions for purposes other than those described above.

4. Sharing of Data

Mental Gym does not share any data with third parties, advertisers, or other services. Data does not leave your device.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete all data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual notes and photos within the app
  • Uninstalling the app — this removes all local data
  • Clearing app data in Android system settings (Settings → Apps → Mental Gym → Clear Data)

6. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 years of age and does not knowingly collect data from such persons. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, contact us — we will immediately delete such information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page. We recommend reviewing it periodically.
